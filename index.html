Why Koh Samet

Koh Samet, the island of pristine beaches

Named after the 'Samet' trees that once flourished on its shores, Ko Samet is a paradise renowned for its pristine beaches and vibrant marine life. Despite its small size, Koh Samet boasts a rich history dating back centuries, with traces of ancient settlements and maritime trade routes.

Today, Koh Samet attracts visitors from around the world with its azure waters and golden sands. The island is part of the Khao Laem Ya-Mu Ko Samet National Park, protecting its diverse ecosystem of coral reefs and tropical forests. Adventure seekers can explore the island's hiking trails, hidden waterfalls, and panoramic viewpoints, while water sports enthusiasts revel in snorkeling, diving, and kayaking opportunities.

Beyond its natural beauty, Koh Samet offers a vibrant nightlife scene, with beachfront bars and restaurants serving up fresh seafood and refreshing cocktails. Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, Koh Samet promises an unforgettable island escape in the heart of the Gulf of Thailand.
